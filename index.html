FPS Game

This is the first real game I made and published. It was made in Unity and I submitted it as a project for my Computer Science (Informatica) class final workpiece in my last year of high school. It's a first person zombie wave shooter and I made it in collaboration with a friend who helped mainly with the models. It taught me a lot about how important having proper leadership is, because the friend who I worked with would slack a lot and I struggled making him stick to our planned deadlines. So in the end I ended up doing 80-90% of the work. Even though this put some stress on me, I still ended up with a nice product and it was even almost submitted by our teacher to be one of the top workpieces that year.

2D Platformer Game

This is the 2nd game I had officially published. It was right after my first one and it was made as an application for BUAS. I however was focused on exams and studying so I didn't make enough time for it. It was still a worthwhile experience and I learned a lot. It was a 2d platformer with 3 levels, I made a story for it and some custom sprites. It was also made in Unity and gave me some more experience working in code and on 2d games.
