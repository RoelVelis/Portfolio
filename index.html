FPS Game

This is the first real game I made and published. It was made in Unity and I submitted it as a project for my Computer Science (Informatica) class final workpiece in my last year of high school. It's a first person zombie wave shooter and I made it in collaboration with a friend who helped mainly with the models. It taught me a lot about how important having proper leadership is, because the friend who I worked with would slack a lot and I struggled making him stick to our planned deadlines. So in the end I ended up doing 80-90% of the work. Even though this put some stress on me, I still ended up with a nice product and it was even almost submitted by our teacher to be one of the top workpieces that year.

School

The elementary school I went to was NBS Boeimeer in Breda. I skipped the first year which caused me to be among the youngest in my classes going forth. I didn't get held back and even took some advanced maths classes when they were available. I ended up graduating with a HAVO/VWO advice.

Middle/High school

I went to Mencia de Mendoza Lyceum in Breda. I studied at a HAVO TTO level meaning I got bilingual courses and got the opportunity to go on multiple cultural trips. Sadly the exchange that was planned got cancelled due to covid. I averaged a steady 7.5/10 throughout my years and eventually went with a study-profile (Curriculum) of E&M (Economics and Social). I graduated without issue and got a IGCSE Certificate for my bilingual and globalisation skills.

College/Uni

I went to Hogeschool Rotterdam for a year. Here I learnt about game design and web development. I went to their Creative Media & Game Technologies course and made some good friends there. After I got my propaedeutic certificate I ended up leaving and taking a gap year as the school was focusing more on web development than game development and web development didn't really interest me as much. Thankfully I did learn a lot in my year there, I got used to working in groups and taking a lead/managerial position in group projects. I got familiar with working for a client. Got a lot better at accessibility, diversity and inclusivity. And learnt a lot about self study and game design.
